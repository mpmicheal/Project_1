"Online security: What does it mean, and how can you defend yourself on the web?"

What is Internet Security

Online security involves protecting digital activities and interactions. It's a key element of both cybersecurity and computer protection, covering web browsing safety, online behavior, and network defense, among other essential areas.

"We spend a significant amount of our time online, and some of the security risks we might face include:"

  • Hacking, where someone without authorized access gains access to people's computer systems and email accounts.
  • Phishing Attacks: These are fraudulent emails or messages designed to trick users into providing personal information, such as passwords or credit card details.
  • Social Engineering: This is where people are manipulated through deceptive tactics into revealing confidential information about themselves.
